Scrum Team

The Team is the set of people who develops the product. They have to implement what the Product Owner decides.

the Team is "cross-functional" and "self-organizing".

"Cross-functional" means that the Team has all the skills necessary in order to build the features required.

"Self-organizing" means that nobody will ever tell the team how to implement the features, not even the ScrumMaster. The Team decides how many items (from the Product Backlog) to build in a Sprint.

Each member of the Team is recognized as a Developer. There are no fixed specialist titles in a group that adopts Scrum.

The Team should have between 3 to 9 people. The Product Owner and the ScrumMaster are not part of the count.
