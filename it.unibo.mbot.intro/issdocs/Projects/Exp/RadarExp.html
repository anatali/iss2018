Radar

Two robots R1 and R2 are exploring a flat space. Each robot is equipped with a radar (on it front size) with a radar. The two robots move along two parallel straight line separated by a (prefixed) distance LD.

While a robot is moving, its radar explores the space in front of it and build a synthetic sensor value (called here 'data') structured as follows: 

    data( ROBOTNAME, posRobot(X,Y,Z), posObj( ANGLE, DISTANCE )
When the radar detects an obstacle, the robot sends the current data value to a remote CentralUnit. The CentralUnit compares the data values of the two robots in order to understand if the two robots are detected the same object. In this case, the CentralUnit send a command to each robot (e.g. to take a photo of the object).
